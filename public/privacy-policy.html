2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Health Data: Period dates, cycle information, symptoms (flow, mood, physical symptoms), temperature readings, and personal notes
  • Usage Data: App usage patterns, feature preferences, and device information
  • Authentication Data: Google Sign-In information (if used)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide period tracking and cycle prediction services
  • Send reminders and notifications about upcoming periods
  • Generate health insights and statistics
  • Improve our app functionality and user experience
  • Communicate important updates about our services

4. Data Security

Your data security is our top priority:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (SSL/TLS) and at rest
  • Secure Storage: Data is stored on Neon PostgreSQL database with industry-standard security
  • Authentication: Secure authentication using JWT tokens and Firebase
  • Access Control: Only you can access your personal health data
  • Data Retention: Your data is retained as long as your account is active

🌐 Cloud Sync

Your data is securely stored in the cloud. This means:

  • Your data won't be lost if you delete the app
  • You can access your data from any device by signing in
  • Your cycle history and symptoms are always backed up

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share data only in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you choose to export and share your data with healthcare providers
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party services (Google Firebase, Neon Database) solely for app functionality
